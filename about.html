Our Identity & Mission

Combining seasoned executive oversight with modern corporate infrastructure to deliver reliable, compliant, and scalable consulting and referral solutions.

20/59 Ventures Corp is a premier, veteran-owned professional consulting and strategic referral firm. We are dedicated to driving operational excellence, regulatory compliance, and structured organizational management across both public and private sectors.

Grounded in a culture of discipline and precision, we specialize in helping organizations navigate complex institutional frameworks, optimize administrative workflows, and facilitate critical housing referral and community-focused initiatives. At 20/59 Ventures, we combine seasoned executive oversight with modern corporate infrastructure to deliver reliable, compliant, and scalable solutions for our partners, financial institutions, and government agencies.

Andrea Marcus

President & Owner

Andrea Marcus brings decades of disciplined leadership, administrative oversight, and regulatory expertise to 20/59 Ventures. As a military veteran and retired state supervisor, Andrea has spent her career mastering the complexities of compliance, systemic management, and institutional frameworks.

As President and founder, she establishes the strategic direction of the firm, ensures strict adherence to corporate governance, and champions the company’s mission-driven community and referral initiatives. Her extensive background in public-sector oversight and operational discipline makes her uniquely equipped to navigate joint ventures, federal contracting systems, and housing authority relationships. Under her leadership, 20/59 Ventures maintains an unwavering commitment to integrity, structure, and reliable execution.
