Phone, SMS & MMS Disclosure

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Please read this Phone, SMS & MMS Disclosure carefully before submitting your phone number to 20 59 Ventures Corp. This document describes how we collect, use, and protect your telephone contact information.

1. Consent & Opt-In Policy

When you submit a contact, referral, partner, or landlord inquiry form on our website and provide a telephone number, you will be given the option to opt-in to receive transactional or informational messages. Consent is entirely voluntary and is never pre-checked or assumed. You must actively check the boxes on our forms to grant consent:

  • Transactional & Support Consent: Consents to receive communications directly relating to your transactions, housing referral applications, or immediate support requests.
  • Informational Updates Consent: Consents to receive notifications regarding housing availability updates, service announcements, and informational updates.

2. Strict Non-Marketing Promise

20 59 Ventures Corp does not send any marketing, promotional, or sales text messages. Our text message (SMS/MMS) programs are strictly limited to transactional coordination (e.g. appointment updates, referral status) and customer support response. We will never use your phone number to pitch or advertise services.

3. Carrier Charges & Mobile Data

Standard message and data rates may apply from your mobile service provider. 20 59 Ventures Corp does not charge you to send or receive messages, but your carrier's standard rates for text messages or data plans still apply. Message frequency varies based on your inquiry, referral activity, and support needs.

4. Opt-Out Options (STOP and HELP)

You can cancel or manage your text message preferences at any time:

To Opt-Out: Reply STOP to any message you receive from us. We will send you a single confirmation text to confirm that you have been unsubscribed, and no further messages will be sent unless you opt back in.

For Help: Reply HELP to any message, or contact our support team at info@2059ventures.online or andrea.marcus@2059ventures.online or call 1-888-919-2059 or 205-534-8492.

5. Phone Information Security & Sharing Exclusion

We value your privacy. Your personal information, including your mobile phone number and SMS consent status, will never be shared, sold, rented, or traded with third parties, affiliates, or partners for marketing, promotional, or commercial purposes under any circumstances.

6. Contact Information

If you have questions or concerns about this disclosure or our mobile messaging program, please reach out to us at:
