Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

1. Introduction

20/59 Ventures ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website and use our housing referral services.

2. SMS & Mobile Information (Important)

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

We will not share your opt-in to an SMS campaign with any third party for purposes unrelated to providing you with the services of that campaign. We may share your Personal Data, including your SMS opt-in or consent status, with third parties that help us provide our messaging services, including but not limited to platform providers, phone companies, and any other vendors who assist us in the delivery of text messages. Under no circumstances will we share SMS consent or phone numbers with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

3. Information Collection

We collect information you provide directly to us when you fill out our forms, request housing placement, apply as a landlord partner, or get in touch. This information may include your name, email address, phone number, veteran status, senior status, and other details related to your housing needs or property.

4. SMS Communications

If you opt-in to receive SMS from us:

  • Opt-In: Consent is obtained through web forms where you explicitly check a box to receive messages.
  • Opt-Out: You can cancel the SMS service at any time. Just text "STOP" to our phone number. We will send you an SMS to confirm you have been unsubscribed, and will no longer send messages to your device.
  • Help: If you experience issues, reply HELP for assistance, or email info@2059ventures.online or andrea.marcus@2059ventures.online.
  • Rates: Standard message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies.

5. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to safeguard your information. However, no electronic transmission is 100% secure. We recommend that you do not send sensitive personal credentials (like social security numbers) over standard text messages.
