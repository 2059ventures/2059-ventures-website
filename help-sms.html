Frequently Asked Questions

How do I opt-out?

Simply reply STOP to any message you receive from us. You will receive a final confirmation text confirming your unsubscribe, and we will no longer send messages to your device unless you opt back in.

What kind of messages will I receive?

You will only receive transactional and informational text messages that you have specifically consented to, such as appointment reminders, support inquiries, and housing referral updates. We do not send marketing or promotional materials.

Are there any costs?

We do not charge any fees to use our SMS service. However, standard message and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply depending on your phone plan.
