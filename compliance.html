E-Verify Participation

20 59 Ventures Corp participates in the federal **E-Verify** program. This electronic database system, managed by the Department of Homeland Security (DHS) and the Social Security Administration (SSA), allows participating employers to verify the identity and employment eligibility of all newly hired employees to work in the United States.

English

Federal law requires all employers to verify the identity and employment eligibility of all persons hired to work in the United States. This organization participates in E-Verify.

Español

La ley federal exige a todos los empleadores que verifiquen la identidad y la elegibilidad de empleo de todas las personas contratadas para trabajar en los Estados Unidos. Esta organización participa en E-Verify.

Right to Work Notice

The Immigrant and Employee Rights Section (IER) of the U.S. Department of Justice (DOJ) enforces the anti-discrimination provisions of the Immigration and Nationality Act. Under this law, it is illegal for employers to discriminate against work-authorized individuals during hiring, firing, or recruitment based on their citizenship status or national origin.

English

If you have the legal right to work in the United States, there are laws that protect you against discrimination. You do not have to be a U.S. citizen to work.

Español

Si tiene el derecho legal de trabajar en los Estados Unidos, existen leyes que lo protegen contra la discriminación. No tiene que ser un ciudadano estadounidense para trabajar.
